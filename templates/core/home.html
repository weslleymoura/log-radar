How does it work?

Online tracking of urban logistics operations

Assuming we are running a logistic company, which is responsible to deliver parcels from a given origin location to a destination. The system will work as follow:


Mapping business patterns

We use density-based contours (Gaussian kernel) to map business patterns. We assume these patterns are the expected behavior of the company operation

Online tracking of the operation

We then track the movement of the parcels across the network

Flagging business anomalies

Parcels which does not match the business patterns are selected for further scrutiny

Tracking the drift of business

The system constantly observe the data distribution by the usage of online clustering models. Whenever is needed, contours (business patterns) are updated
